PotSpot
Snooker ball detector for online snooker streams

About

PotSpot detects the table and balls in snooker livestreams and renders an interactive top-down plan view of the table. Click any ball to see aim lines and object-ball deflections.

More information and privacy policy at potspot.net

Detection runs entirely in the browser — no data leaves your device apart from completely anonymous usage stats (basically, how many times Scan gets clicked).

Accuracy

The resolution of a stream has fewer pixels than a snooker table has millimetres. Therefore balls might be very slightly off: there's nothing we can do about that apart from demand 4k streams and watch them on 4k monitors.

Accuracy is mostly affected by poor quality blurry video, which happens either if you have low bandwidth or you're watching cheap coverage (eg snooker900.tv): the extension will warn you if video isn't crisp. If you see baulk colours slightly drift off their spots on some scans, this is almost always down to video quality.
